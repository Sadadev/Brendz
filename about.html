ABOUT US

WHO ARE WE?

BRENDZ Professional Services (BRENDZ) was incorporated on 15 November 2016 to provide end to end consultancy services on expatriate mobility and residency. We offer a bouquet of services which are designed to facilitate compliance with extant laws and regulations on expatriate mobility and residency in Nigeria. We also offer specialized services that are customized to assist expatriate employees settle down in their new environment. Our service offerings are both advisory and compliance in nature.
At BRENDZ, we are client focused. We work closely with our clients to increase their readiness/ efficiency level as they strive to deliver on the core of their businesses, while at the same time maintaining an inclusive work environment. Our team of professionals combines talent, quality, and values to deliver excellent consultancy services.
Our expatriate mobility and residency services are designed to meet the outsourcing needs of modern day business across all industries within the Nigeria business environment. BRENDZ’s differentiation is in its focused business model and specialized management consultancy service portfolio that makes the firm a unique, credible and an unbiased service provider.
We think about our clients’ needs, and so from the very beginning BRENDZ Professional Services focus its work on exceeding the expectation of its clients. We also find it crucial to adapt to the peculiarity of each client without undermining integrity, our watch word.

Our Work Team

Name: Lucky Sada
Position: Partner and Head
Years of experience: 22 years

Lucky Sada is the leading Partner in the firm. He has 16 years extensive experience working with KPMG Professional Services and 3 years experience with Deloitte Nigeria. Prior to joining KPMG in 2001, Lucky worked with BACRAL Consulting, a Business and Management consulting firm, for 2 years. He has provided numerous advisory and compliance services to various organizations in different industries and subsectors including the consumer/manufacturing markets, aviation, oil and gas, power, telecommunications, banking, information technology as well as construction and advertising. Lucky is a graduate of University of Benin. He holds a Master of Science degree in Public Administration, and a Bachelor of Science degree in Political Science and Public Administration. Lucky is an Affiliate Member – Chartered Institute of Personnel Management of Nigeria (CIPM). Lucky was a member of the technical team constituted by the Ministry of Interior to midwife the current Handbook on Expatriate Quota Administration, revised edition 2022. Lucky also served as a subject matter expert on the Technical Committee that was set up by Nigeria Employers’ Consultative Association (NECA) in 2010. The committee was mandated to articulate the issues surrounding the challenges faced by stakeholders in obtaining facilities at the Ministry of Interior and subsequently meet with the Honorable Minister to present NECA’s position with the view to facilitating efficiency in the application process.
