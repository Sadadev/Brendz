We assist our clients with the documentation and processes leading to the issuance of the under listed immigration facilities required for expatriate mobility to Nigeria:

  • Tourist Visa
    • Required for tourism and short family visits.
  • Business Visa
    • Required for business discussions/ meetings.
  • Visa on arrival
    • Required for top business executive who are unable to apply for business visa at the Nigerian Mission in their home country due to tight travel schedule.
  • TWP Visas
    • TWP visas are issued to expatriates required for short term projects (installation, training and commissioning work etc.) in Nigeria. The nature of the assignments must be very technical such that the skills required cannot be sourced locally.
  • Subject to Regulariation of Visas
    • Entry visa required for long term employment
  • Consular Support Services
    • This entails following up with the Embassy until the visa is issued. We also assist with preparing responses to queries raised at any point during the application process.
  • Meet and Greet Services
    • Airport expediting
