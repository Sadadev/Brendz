CULTURAL INTEGRATION SERVICES

We pay a lot of attention to our clients’ needs and demands. This plays a significant role in how we approach each engagement. Our mandate is to exceed clients’ expectations through an efficient and cost saving approach.
From interactions with expatriates at different occasions, we discovered that very few of them are excited at the prospect of relocating to Nigeria for employment when contacted. Some of them are heavily inundated with negative perceptions about the country to the extent that they turn down employment opportunities in Nigeria.
Our cultural integration services are designed to assist and Assignee get acquainted with the cultural values, various social amenities and other auxiliary facilities available to make his stay an enjoyable and memorable one.
BRENDZ will work closely with HR team to analyze the immediate needs of a new Assignee as he prepares to relocate to Nigeria and customize a robust session that will satisfy the identified needs. This service involves the following tasks:

Look and See

This is a pre-assignment visit package. It is usually customized based on the information provided on the need assessment questionaire completed by the Assignee. The questionaire covers all areas of needs that will provide an insight into what the expatriate will require to facikitate his familiarization with the new environment.

Cultural Integration Session

This package is designed to provide useful information that will facilitate a seamless integration into the Nigerian socio-economic milieu. Nigeria is a heterogeneous and complex society. Our cultural integration session will address some of the initial challenges the new Assignee might face. The session provides vital information that will extol the diverse cultural heritage in the heritage in the country, provide vital tips that will assist the expatriate adapt to the new work environment and facilitate his speedy settlement into the prevailing social lifestyle.

The discussion is usually very robust.
