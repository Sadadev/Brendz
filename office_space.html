RESIDENTIAL/OFFICE SPACE SOURCING

We recognized that searching for good properties for residential and office purposes can be time consuming. We have the right competencies and connections to provide you with rental solutions.
In rendering this service, BRENDZ will work with identified property agents to facilitate property selection and letting process. This can be as an integrated joint service or a completely outsourced one. This service involves the following tasks:

  • Analysing the brief from a client and seeking appropriate information to make the brief complete
  • Liasing with credible property agents and realtors to narrow down the search.
  • Providing a shortlist of identified properties
  • Conducting joint inspections with Assignee/client rep.
  • Assisting with the review of terms and conditions of lease, and rent negotiations.
  • Liasing with property owner/manager to finalize the lease and handover of keys.
